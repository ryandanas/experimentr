We are attempting to specify a Allowed Grader Policy according to our desired goals. Using the dropdown menus below, make a single change so that all of our goals are satisfied.

The Allowed Grader Policy consists of Persons, Classes and Assignments.

A Person can be a Student or a Professor.

There is who is the instructor of each Class.

There is who are the assistants for each Class.

There is who are enrolled in each Class.

There is associated with each Assignment.

There is assigned to each Assignment.

Each Student assigned to an Assignment is enrolled in the Class associated with the Assignment.

The assistants for and the instructor of a Class can grade Assignments associated with the Class.

Some of our goals are not satisfied.

There must be exactly one instructor of every Class.

There must be exactly one Class associated with each Assignment.

Each Student assigned to an Assignment must be enrolled in the Class associated with the Assignment.

The assistants for and the instructor of a Class must grade Assignments associated with the Class.

There is no Student who can grade an Assignment they are assigned to.
